About Me

Hi! I’m a mobile app developer specializing in cross-platform development and modern digital solutions. For me, technology is not just a job but a way of life, where I constantly explore new tools and approaches. In my projects, I focus on usability, functionality, and high performance, creating products that truly make a difference.

Beyond development, I have a deep passion for automotive design. I love working with carbon fiber to create custom parts such as unique mirrors, splitters, and body kits. Blending technology with aesthetics excites me—I enjoy experimenting with materials and shapes to craft not just functional but also visually striking components.

I’m also an avid traveler and have explored 13 countries so far, discovering different cultures, architectures, and technological advancements worldwide. Traveling inspires me with new ideas and helps me view development and design from fresh perspectives.

Accompanying me on this journey is my beloved Akita—a fluffy bundle of joy who makes every day better.
